About Me!

My name is Aaron Friedman, and I am a detail oriented individual with a tremendous passion for communications and IT work. I am currently entering my senior year at Ridgewood High School. I have a proven track record in designing websites, organizing, editing videos and managing social media accounts. I have strong technical skills as well as excellent interpersonal skills, enabling me to interact with a wide range of stakeholders. I am eager to be challenged in order to grow and further improve my IT and creative skills. My greatest passion is using my technical know-how to benefit other people and organizations.

Video Editor, Mike Siegel for Congress

My videos have amassed thousands of views across Twitter and Instagram.

Creator, Firebrand Media

Edited, designed, and created original content on Instagram. Consulted for various organizations and campaigns on website work and video.

Co-founder, Ridgewood High School Democrats

Near the end of sophomore year, I immersed myself in the political sphere, becoming a fellow for two local State Assembly campaigns. I co-founded and led the High School Democrats of America chapter at my school (one of the largest chapters in the state). Instrumental in coordinating robust canvassing efforts with 70+ students, I have assembled a massive database of names and phone numbers and sent out nightly information blasts. Because the majority of students do not have their driver’s license, I helped coordinate fleets of transportation to make these efforts possible.

Former Communications Director, New Jersey High School Democrats

In only one year, I have entirely revamped New Jersey High School Democrat’s outreach and multiplied every digital engagement metric.
