About Me

I have a proven track record in designing websites, organizing, editing videos and managing social media accounts. I have strong technical skills as well as excellent interpersonal skills, enabling me to interact with a wide range of stakeholders. I am extremely passionate in using my technical know-how to benefit progressive candidates and causes.
